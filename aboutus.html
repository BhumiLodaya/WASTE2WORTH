About Us

At Subhadra Enterprises, we believe sustainability is not just a goal—it’s a responsibility. We are dedicated to creating solutions that serve both industry and agriculture, ensuring progress goes hand in hand with care for the planet.
Industrial Solutions
Steel Sector Solutions
High-strength briquettes using sinter fines, LD sludge, and cement
Resource Optimization
Better use of industrial by-products for efficient operations
Cleaner Operations
Supporting cleaner and more efficient furnace operations
Agricultural Excellence
On the agricultural side, we bring nature closer to farming through our premium cocopeat, made from coconut husks. Loved by growers in horticulture, floriculture, and hydroponics, cocopeat provides a natural, eco-friendly medium that helps plants thrive.
Premium Cocopeat
Made from coconut husks for sustainable growing
Eco-Friendly Medium

Plant-Friendly
